Mid-Century Midtown Lounge

Room Description

In desirable midtown Florence Park neighborhood! Near Cherry Street. 4 min. to downtown. Has regular-sized full matress now. Nice recently-remodeled studio with Mid-Century furniture! Separate part of brick home. Full spacious bathroom with shower. Free wifi! Free Netflix! 60" TV!

Free Netflix: Yes Laundry: On Request

Very nice recently-remodeled studio apartment which is in excellent condition and has been fully remodeled. Studio is 350 square feet. 60" LCD Smart TV. 2 beds: full-size futon with 6" memory foam mattress & single roll-away memory foam bed. Gorgeous spacious bathroom with large shower.

Free wifi! Free Netflix! SmartTV with Netflix/Hulu, Amazon Prime and more! 60" flat-screen LCD TV! Free Netflix access included!

This is a studio apartment or "mother-in-law suite" which is part of a classic brick cottage-style house in a quiet walkable neighborhood. It has a separate keyed-entry through a foyer. The studio is in excellent condition and has been fully remodeled (all new plumbing, electricity, walls, bathroom and flooring).

Kitchenette includes: Mini refrigerator, microwave, Toaster oven, hot plate (can cook an egg), coffee maker, along with a few dishes and cutlery.

Futon bed is full size. It has a comfy memory foam mattress which gets fresh clean fitted sheets for each reservation (along with fresh clean pillow cases and duvet cover). Clean towels are also provided.

We offer a discount for TU students (If you are a TU student, just write for a special discounted rate!).

Guests will have full private use of the studio with all of the amenities listed. The apartment has a separate-entry through a foyer. Guests will access to the fenced back yard.

Laundry service on request! Also, newly refurbished laundromat is a couple blocks away, University of Wash, which also has a bar, arcade and freshly prepared hot sandwiches.

Parking is on the safe quiet neighborhood street with little traffic.

There are two inside dogs in the main house who will be out in the yard from time to time, but they are well mannered and should not affect your stay (They are quiet 99% of the time and we have never had a complaint about them. Furthermore the shared wall, is nearly 2 feet thick brick). Let us know if you need to bring pets. We would ask for a pet fee/deposit.

Please note that there is no thermostat in the Lounge. The temperature is kept between 70-72F. Please let us know after check-in if any adjustment is needed and we will do our best to accommodate you.
